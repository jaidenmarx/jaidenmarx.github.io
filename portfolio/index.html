About Me

I am a first year, Digital Media Arts student at San Jose State University. I was born and raised in San Francisco and I make vibrant and dynamic work using design, photography, animation, and interactive media. My process is very explorative. I like to try out different mediums and see which one fits each idea best. I am inspired by variety and explore how different art forms can connect to create something new. Through this mix of mediums, I hope to spark curiosity and encourage people to see the world in fresh ways. I am also interested in exploring how art and social justice can be connected and the possibilities to invoke positive change through art.​
