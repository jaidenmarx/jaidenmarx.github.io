The "United" States of America

I made this series because in the world today, there is a lot of division between people. I wanted to show that even though we are supposed to be one people, division is supported in today's society. The idea of unity and trust is broken and is something that needs to be addressed. I wanted to show it in a way that shows division itself and a little uncomfortable to look at so I made the images far away from each other. ​
